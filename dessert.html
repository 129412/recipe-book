1. Dark Chocolate Avocado Mousse

Servings Prep Time Cook Time
2–3 10 mins 0 mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ¼ cup honey or maple syrup
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Optional: dark chocolate shavings, berries, chopped nuts

Instructions:

  1. Place avocados, cocoa powder, honey/maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Taste and adjust sweetness if needed.
  4. Spoon into bowls and chill for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Garnish with chocolate shavings, berries, or nuts before serving.

Tip: Add almond butter or coconut cream for extra richness.

2. Oats and Nuts Pudding

Servings Prep Time Cook Time
2 5 mins 5 mins

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1–2 tsp honey or maple syrup
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon powder
  • 2 tbsp chopped mixed nuts
  • Optional: dried fruits, berries, chia seeds

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, combine oats, milk, and cinnamon.
  2. Cook for 4–5 minutes until soft and pudding-like.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in honey/maple syrup.
  4. Pour into bowls and add chopped nuts.
  5. Add optional toppings and serve warm or chilled.

Tip: Soak oats overnight for a quick no-cook version.
