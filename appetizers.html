WHOLE WHEAT VEG MINI WRAPS

Servings Prep Time Cook Time
4 15 mins 10 mins

Ingredients:

  • 4 whole wheat tortillas (small size)
  • 1 cup mixed bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 1 small carrot, grated
  • ½ cucumber, julienned
  • ½ cup baby spinach leaves
  • ¼ cup hummus or Greek yogurt (for spread)
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: fresh herbs like cilantro or parsley

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté bell peppers and carrot for 3–4 minutes until slightly tender. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Warm the whole wheat tortillas slightly to make them flexible.
  3. Spread a thin layer of hummus or Greek yogurt over each tortilla.
  4. Layer the sautéed vegetables, cucumber, spinach, and herbs evenly on each tortilla.
  5. Roll the tortillas tightly into wraps and cut each into 2–3 mini wraps.
  6. Serve immediately or refrigerate for a light, healthy snack later.

Tip:

You can add roasted chickpeas or a sprinkle of feta for extra protein and flavor.

BAKED SWEET POTATO FRIES

Servings Prep Time Cook Time
2-3 10 mins 25 mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into fries
  • 1–2 tsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • ¼ tsp salt (or to taste)
  • Optional: pinch of chili flakes for heat

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, toss the sweet potato fries with olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, salt, black pepper, and chili flakes (if using).
  3. Make sure they are evenly coated.
  4. Spread the fries on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper in a single layer.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes, flip the fries, and bake for another 12–15 minutes until crispy and golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite healthy dip, such as yogurt-based dip, hummus, or tomato salsa.

Tip:

For extra crispiness, soak the cut sweet potatoes in cold water for 30 minutes before baking, then pat them dry.
