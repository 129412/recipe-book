Healthy Beverages

Welcome to our collection of healthy beverages designed to keep you refreshed and energized throughout the day.

We have 2 healthy drink options and the recipes are given below.

1. Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

Servings Prep Time Cook Time
2 5 mins 0 mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 tablespoons natural peanut butter
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or any milk of choice)
  • ½ cup plain Greek yogurt (optional for creaminess)
  • 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • A pinch of cinnamon (optional)
  • Ice cubes (as needed)

Instructions:

  1. Peel the bananas and cut them into chunks.
  2. In a blender, combine bananas, peanut butter, almond milk, Greek yogurt, and honey/maple syrup.
  3. Add a few ice cubes for a chilled smoothie.
  4. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  5. Pour into glasses, sprinkle with a pinch of cinnamon if desired, and serve immediately.

Tip: Add a tablespoon of chia seeds or flaxseeds for an extra nutritional boost.


2. Watermelon Cooler

Servings Prep Time Cook Time
2–3 10 mins 0 mins

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups seedless watermelon, cubed
  • ½ cup cold water or coconut water
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1–2 teaspoons honey or agave syrup (optional)
  • A few fresh mint leaves
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Place watermelon cubes, cold water/coconut water, lime juice, and honey/agave in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Strain the mixture through a fine sieve if you prefer a smoother texture.
  4. Pour into glasses over ice cubes.
  5. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and serve.

Tip: For a sparkling version, add a splash of soda water before serving for a fizzy twist.
